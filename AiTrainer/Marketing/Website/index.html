About AI Trainer

AI Trainer is a collectible adventure where you explore the world, gather computer parts, and build robots to compete and learn through play. Play as a young aspiring engineer with more ideas than resources. Build, repair, and refine your own machines that learn from failure and grow stronger through experience.
